Part 4 — Your Audience
Who you're writing for and what you refuse to talk about.
Q11

Be specific — not "business leaders." Who exactly? What do they struggle with?

Part 5 — Your Brand Voice
How you write, what formats feel natural, and the legacy you want to leave.
Q14

Q16

Not the company's mission statement. Your actual, personal answer.
